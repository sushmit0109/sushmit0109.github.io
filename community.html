Bengali.AI and public-interest research

Bengali.AI is a community research initiative (8,000+ researchers) focused on democratizing Bengali language technology through open datasets, reproducible benchmarks, and research competitions.

Selected initiatives

  • Launched a Kaggle research competition on Bengali speech recognition (prize pool: USD 53,000).
  • Crowdsourced large Bengali speech corpora via Mozilla Common Voice and designed multi-domain test datasets.
  • Co-designed annotation guidelines for Bengali document layout analysis (object-level segmentation, 40k images).
  • Led development of Bangladeshi Sign Language dataset and the Bornil crowdsourcing platform.
  • Worked on Indic Unicode normalization and open-source normalization/parsing algorithms.
  • Supervised 20+ research groups across Bangladeshi universities; organised multiple competitions and raised funding/waivers.
