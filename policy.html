Policy, governance, and public-sector delivery

I work on the design of technology governance instruments—laws, policies, standards-aligned controls, and institutional mechanisms— and on translating them into implementable programmes and systems.

National AI Policy (2026–2030)

Member, National AI Policy Steering Committee (ICT Division). Contributed to governance frameworks, interoperability and data mechanisms, risk classification, regulatory sandboxes, public-sector deployment guidance, and institutional oversight.

Drafting and legal design

Led and contributed to drafting national legal instruments including: Cyber Safety Ordinance (2025), Personal Data Protection Ordinance (2025), National Data Governance & Interoperability Authority (NDGIA) Ordinance (2025), Postal Service Ordinance (2025), Bangladesh Telecommunication Ordinance (2025), and Hi‑Tech Authority (Amendment) Ordinance (2025).

Conducted comparative analysis of international laws/standards and synthesised multi‑stakeholder inputs into implementable controls.

Assurance and oversight

Conducted organisation-wide assessments, risk registers, and decision-ready roadmaps for senior stakeholders; reviewed system designs to identify trust-boundary issues, access control gaps, and operational risks in citizen-facing platforms.
