About

I am a policy technologist and data scientist in Bangladesh’s digital transformation ecosystem. My work includes legal and policy drafting (privacy, data governance, cyber safety, telecom), public-sector system design, and technical audit/oversight of national-scale services. I also maintain an active research track record in applied machine learning and have led community initiatives that democratise Bengali language technologies through open datasets and competitions.

Research

My research spans medical imaging, speech and language technologies, and data-centric AI. I care about reproducibility, dataset quality, evaluation rigor, and systems that remain accountable under real-world constraints.
