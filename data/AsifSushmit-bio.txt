Asif Shahriyar Sushmit is a policy technologist and data scientist working at the intersection of AI research, technology governance, and public-sector digital transformation in Bangladesh. His work spans national-scale system design and oversight, legislative and policy drafting (data governance, privacy, cyber safety, telecom), and community-led research initiatives through Bengali.AI, where he has coordinated large open datasets and competitions. He has published in medical imaging and language technologies and aims to pursue graduate study (MS/PhD) to advance research on AI governance, data systems, and institutional design for public value.
